# Prosper: Wealth Architecture for the Modern Driver

## 🎯 The Soul Purpose
The core mission of **Prosper** is to facilitate a radical **identity shift**: transforming a person who "drives for money" into a **Strategic Business Owner** who builds an empire.

In the current transportation landscape, many drivers suffer from "The Invisible Leak"—where cash flows in and out of their pockets daily without a trace, leaving them perpetually stuck in a cycle of survival. Prosper is the digital intervention designed to break that cycle.

---

## 🏛️ The Three Pillars of the Experience

### 1. The Perspective Shift (Stoic Financial Discipline)
Prosper is built on the Stoic principle of focusing on what you can control. A driver cannot control fuel prices or rider traffic, but they can control their **Net Profit Margin**.
* **From Gross to Net:** The app forces the driver to see the difference between the cash in their hand and the wealth they actually own.
* **Mindset Reframing:** By categorizing expenses as "Leaks" or "Investments," the app discourages impulsive daily spending in favor of long-term stability.

### 2. Wealth Architecture (Beyond Savings)
Most apps track "what happened." Prosper tracks **"what is becoming."** * **Annual Vision:** It turns abstract numbers into a tangible roadmap. It answers the question: *"If I keep doing exactly what I did today, where will I be in 5 years?"*
* **The Safety Net:** It replaces fear with security. By quantifying "Days of Freedom" instead of just a balance, it gives the driver the psychological breathing room to handle life’s shocks.

### 3. Professional Credibility (The Data Bridge)
For the informal worker, "proof of income" is the greatest barrier to growth.
* **Asset Management:** By tracking maintenance and vehicle health, the app treats the car as a high-value business asset rather than just a tool.
* **Insight Reporting:** Prosper generates professional-grade financial statements that turn a driver's daily grind into a credible history for banks, car dealers, and investors.

---

## 🛠️ The Technical "Why"
As a full-stack solution, the app's architecture reflects its purpose:
* **Offline-First (MMKV/SQLite):** Because business doesn't stop when the network does.
* **Django & Supabase Backend:** To ensure that the "Annual Vision" is backed by industrial-grade security and permanent storage.
* **Bento-Grid UI:** High-fidelity data visualization inspired by top-tier professional dashboards (Testora), making financial management feel premium and rewarding.
